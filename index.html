Modding KotOR on iOS

GETTING STARTED:

· Install Sideloadly from https://sideloadly.io/

· Install AppDump2 (https://onejailbreak.com/blog/appdump2/) and TrollStore (https://trollstore.app/) on your iOS device.

· Use AppDump2 and trollstore.app to decrypt KOTOR's IPA, and then use your favorite file manager to send the ipa over to your PC. If this was successful, skip over to MODDING SECTION:

IPATOOL INSTRUCTIONS:

· If for some reason your device doesn't let you install appdump2 or trollstore, here is another way to get the ipa.

· Follow the instructions at https://github.com/majd/ipatool to get yourself signed in. General syntax is:

ipatool auth login --email <youremail> --password <yourpassword>

without the < and >

· Download the k1 or k2 ipa using ipatool:

· ipatool download -b <bundle id>

The bundle ids for both games are as follows:


k1: com.aspyr.kotor.ios

k2: com.aspyr.kotor2.ios

So downloading k1 would be `ipatool download -b com.aspyr.kotor.ios`

Once it's done you may get an error saying it was unable to close a tmp file because it was in use. In the same folder as ipatool.exe, you'll see a .ipa.tmp file, just rename it and remove the '.tmp' extension so it ends with '.ipa'.

if you've gone the ipatool route you'll need these decrypted files:

- k1: https://mega.nz/file/4GBRWaxK#yHQwJAcrgwp47S3BA-SVbub2cugtVO_VzrkbOR5WDuI

- k2: https://mega.nz/file/BORCTbLL#JdLVserxTwD44tfE3K9KscoIArrC5Qg4WMoPGDCmYvE

Now extract the ipa you downloaded with ipatool using 7zip. Delete any SC_Info folders you find (there should be at least 2, maybe 3).

Extract and Move the contents of the above mega.nz download into where you extracted the ipa, overwriting when prompted. If you did not get an overwrite message, check that you put it in the right place.

You now have a decrypted IPA that can be modded and sideloaded to your iOS device. Move on to MODDING SECTION

MODDING your decrypted ipa:

You now have a decrypted ipa that is ready to be modded. Simply point tslpatcher towards that dir (you may need to create a fake file named swkotor.exe so it'll recognize it as a valid game path) and copy your mods into Override etc.

When you are done, ensure you ran the case-sensitivity fix, and note that this should ONLY be ran in the MODULES, OVERRIDE, STREAMWAVES, STREAMSOUNDS, STREAMVOICE, RIMS, or LIPS directory. Do NOT run it in the top level folder of your ipa.

Now that you've lowercased all the files/folders, use your favorite archiving tool to zip everything up. Select all files/folders in the top level and add to an archive. Make sure you don't have an extra folder inside your archive: the top level of your archive should not be a single folder, you should visibly be able to see all the contents.

Skip over to the SIDELOADLY section.

SIDELOADLY:

You are now ready to move your ipa over to your iOS device. Open up Sideloadly, browse for the ipa you've been working with, and press the Advanced Options section.

· Ensure Signing mode is set to 'Apple ID Sideload'. Haven't tried the others.

· Check the 'Enable File-sharing' box. Is relevant for a future tutorial.

· Check 'Remove limitation on supported devices'

· Check the 'Try to support older iOS versions' box.

Checking the last two allowed me to play KOTOR on my iPad Air, despite the app store saying that device was incompatible.

Now plug your iOS device into your PC and select it at the top of Sideloadly (where it says iDevice). Press the bottom right button that looks like two arrows to open the log viewer, and press 'Start'.

· This process will take A WHILE. Grab a coffee, make some popcorn, kiss your girlfriend, do some hw, and come back.

If you see an error that looks like this, you need more free space on your iOS device. From what I understand you need twice the amount of space of the IPA itself. So if your IPA is 5 GB, you need 10GB free, if it's 2GB you need 4 etc.

"""

Got error: Call to lockdownd_start_service failed: LOCKDOWN_E_MUX_ERROR. IPA file exists, retrying

FAILED: Call to lockdownd_start_service failed: LOCKDOWN_E_MUX_ERROR

"""

Sometimes unplugging your iOS device or restarting Sideloadly can fix it but mainly you'll just want to let it do its thing, it continually will retry until it succeeds and it probably does that for a reason.

If you get the 100% in the logviewer, you've successfully modded KOTOR on iOS. Here's a few things to know right off the bat.

· You need to resign the ipa every 7 days unless you have a paid apple developer account. This is an apple restriction for creating iOS apps. Just plug your phone in and let sideloadly sign it automatically every once in a while.

· There is currently a bug with the neocities modbuild and some blaster icons. We are uncertain why this happens.

· If you get an immediate crash after pressing 'new game', you forgot to do the case-sensitivity fixes for iOS. Go back to your pc and redo those steps.
